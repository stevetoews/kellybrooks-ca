<p>I’ll never get used to Vancouver “winter”. Growing up in northern BC, there were no signs of Spring until April. Yesterday I checked out my garden to find the Snowdrops up, and Hellebore and Witchhazel blooming. It never ceases to amaze me and trick me into thinking Spring is just around the corner.</p>
