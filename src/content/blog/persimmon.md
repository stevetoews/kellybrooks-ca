I’ve never eaten one. I looked up how to prepare a persimmon for eating. I haven’t tried this yet.</p>
<p>1 Wash the persimmon.</p>
<p>2 Using a sharp knife cut the top(leaves) off like you would prepare a tomato.</p>
<p>3 Cut it into any shape you desire and then eat it.</p>
<p>4 Persimmons can be cooked or eaten raw, can be made into a jam/marmalade or also be used in salads.</p>
