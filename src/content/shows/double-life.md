*Double Life*, A Pentimenti Group show, July 23, 2015 opening night at the Port Moody Arts Centre, Port Moody, BC.

This group show featured the work of 6 individuals who share professional backgrounds as graphic designers as well as a deep personal creative drive to paint.

The group is known as Pentimenti and members work in a variety of styles, sizes and subject matter, including mixed media, still life, narrative, geometric and organic abstraction.
