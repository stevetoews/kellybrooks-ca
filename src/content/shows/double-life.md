<p><em>Double Life</em>, A Pentimenti Group show, July 23, 2015 opening night at the Port Moody Arts Centre, Port Moody, BC.</p>
<p>This group show featured the work of 6 individuals who share professional backgrounds as graphic designers as well as a deep personal creative drive to paint.</p>
<p>The group is known as Pentimenti and members work in a variety of styles, sizes and subject matter, including mixed media, still life, narrative, landscape, geometric and organic abstraction.</p>
